The Magic of Pencils

February 1, 2026

I love how simple colored pencils are. They’re really easy to use and control, but you can still make your drawings look deep and bright. In this post, I’m looking at how mixing different colors can make a flat drawing look much more real.

I just started trying out "layering," where I draw lightly at first and then keep adding more color on top. It makes the texture look more natural and handmade, which is something that’s hard to get when you’re drawing on a computer.

Acrylic Markers

January 28, 2026

I love using acrylic markers because they are so easy to use. They aren't messy like regular paint, but you still get those really bright and bold colors. In this post, I’m showing how swapping out plain markers for acrylics can make a simple sketch pop.

I’ve been practicing how to layer them by letting one color dry before adding another on top. It gives the art a cool, thick texture that feels way more "real" than a digital drawing. It’s a fun way to make my art look more professional without a lot of extra supplies.
